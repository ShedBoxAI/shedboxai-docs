### 2. Dagster

**Best for:** Asset-centric data platforms

Similar to Prefect in many ways, but with a different mental model.

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|

| Good dev experience | |

### 3. Apache Airflow

**Best for:** Enterprise with existing investment

The original workflow orchestrator, still widely used.

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Huge ecosystem | Complex setup |

| Many operators | Heavy infrastructure |

**Best for:** Long-running workflows
